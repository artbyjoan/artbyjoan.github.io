Art Lessons

Series of lessons and projects from my Drawing and Painting, Communication Design and Ceramics students

    Impressionism Project (Drawing and Painting)
    In this lesson students chose an impressionist masterpainter, conducted research on them and made a replication of the master painting. Once they were completed with the project they gave a short presentation to the class sharing background of the artist and their artwork

Website Design (Communication Design)
Students learned about creating websites that are user-friendly, well organized and creative. Each website had to serve a specific purpose. Students chose to promote their imaginary business, social issues and more. Students used Photoshop and Illustrator to create their design assets for their website and then used dreamweaver to develop the page. Many of the students were proud of their website and decided to maintain their website even after the project.

Cultural Food Project (Ceramics)
Students created a ceramic piece of food that was meaningful to them culturally

Creating a Classroom Knowledgebase through Formative Assessment
Students took ownership of their own learning and classroom by contributing to the Knowledgebase Wall
